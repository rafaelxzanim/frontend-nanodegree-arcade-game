#### How to play
----------------------------
- Open ```index.html```
- Choose your avatar player
- Try to reach the other side of the road
- Avoid the bugs 
- Try to complete the GEM STATUS
- Hearts give you extra lifes
- Rocks stop you 
- The game has 21 levels
